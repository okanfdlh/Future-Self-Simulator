# Video Generation Prompts - Pola Tidur

Dokumentasi prompt yang digunakan untuk generate video menggunakan PixVerse V6.

## Karakter Konsisten (Base)
**Protagonis**: 25-year-old gender-neutral young professional, big city apartment, minimalist style, smartwatch.
**Style**: Cinematic realistic, 35mm, shallow depth.

---

## 1. Positive Outcome (Tidur Cukup)
**Mood**: Warm, bright, hopeful, energetic.

| Segment | Prompt |
|---------|--------|
| Prolog | Warm bedroom lamp, 22:45. A 25-year-old gender-neutral young professional closes a laptop after a work notification, calm expression. Cinematic realistic, 35mm, shallow depth, slow push-in, warm grade. |
| Decision | Same bedroom. The protagonist places the phone out of reach, enables focus mode, takes a slow breath, dims the light. Intimate close-ups, soft highlights, gentle camera sway, warm grade. |
| Montage 1 | Early morning city park. The protagonist jogs lightly, steady breathing, sunrise rim light. Tracking shot, smooth motion, warm and bright, realistic cinematic. |
| Montage 2 | Modern office. The protagonist presents confidently in a meeting, colleagues attentive. Medium-to-wide shot, subtle handheld, warm neutral office light, focused energy. |
| Montage 3 | Quick montage: health check results are good, then dinner laughter with friends/partner. Fast cuts, warm restaurant light, optimistic rhythm, cinematic realistic. |
| Snapshot 1 | Five years later morning. 06:30 sunlight through curtains, the protagonist wakes refreshed, stretches, pours coffee. Calm pacing, soft warm grade, gentle dolly. |
| Snapshot 2 | Office day. The protagonist leads a meeting smoothly, clear focus, relaxed smile. Clean composition, controlled camera, warm neutral grade. |
| Snapshot 3 | Evening golden hour. Light run along the river, then cooking at home with warm laughter. Alternating shots, warm highlights, calm confidence. |
| Closing | Night balcony city lights. Close-up of a calm face, slow exhale, peaceful stillness. Cinematic realistic, warm grade, lingering final frame, reflective tone. |

## 2. Neutral Outcome (Tidur Tidak Konsisten)
**Mood**: Neutral, flat, slightly desaturated.

| Segment | Prompt |
|---------|--------|
| Base | Cinematic realistic, neutral slightly desaturated grade, multi-shot. Protagonist: 25-year-old gender-neutral big-city professional. Prolog: 00:30 phone glow scrolling in bed, tired eyes; cut to next night 22:30 falling asleep early. Habit stays inconsistent: sometimes sleeps now, sometimes delays. 35mm, shallow depth, subtle handheld. |

Big city life, stable but uneven energy. 35mm, subtle handheld. |

| Closing | Cinematic realistic, neutral slightly desaturated grade. Night interior, protagonist looks at a busy calendar and unfinished tasks, quiet exhale, mild fatigue. Subtle city ambience, 35mm, minimal movement. End on reflective still frame. |

## 3. Negative Outcome (Kurang Tidur)
**Mood**: Cold, green tint, harsh contrast, tense.

| Segment | Prompt |
|---------|--------|
| Prolog | Cinematic realistic, cold green tint, harsh contrast, multi-shot. Protagonist: 25-year-old gender-neutral big-city professional. Prolog 02:40: laptop screen harsh glow, red eyes, cold coffee, urgent notifications. Decision: keeps working, sleeps 3–4 hours, brutal alarm, body heavy. 35mm, tight close-ups, tense handheld. |
| Montage | Cinematic realistic, cold green tint, harsh contrast, multi-shot. Montage across 5 years: exhausted commute, unfocused meeting, mistake under pressure, manager disappointed, health check shows insomnia and high stress, small conflict with partner or friend, messy room. 35mm, quick tense cuts, low drone mood. |
| Snapshot | Cinematic realistic, cold green tint, multi-shot. Five years later day: wakes with headache in messy room, shaky hands, midday panic and mistakes, endless notifications, night insomnia loop—lying awake staring at ceiling despite exhaustion. 35mm, claustrophobic framing, tense handheld. |
| Closing | Cinematic realistic, cold green tint. Closing: protagonist sits alone in dark room with laptop glow, heavy breathing, empty silence after city noise fades. Slow push-in, 35mm, harsh highlights, then near stillness. |
